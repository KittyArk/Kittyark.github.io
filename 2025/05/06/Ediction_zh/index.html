Ediction

开发者: Don Attico, Maurimo, gyrium
类型: Visual Novel, Interactive Fiction
平台: Windows, Linux, Android，IOS
链接: https://donattico.itch.io/ediction

游戏简介

Raised from the dead, a man whose life was riddled with pain is made to consider what his last words will be once again.
从死中复生，一个生命充满痛苦的人被迫再次思考他的遗言将是什么。

Ediction is a visual novel made for the Love + Attraction Jam 2025. It is a complete story, sitting at around 23k words. Please note that it contains and discusses heavy themes, particularly suicide, but also death, abuse, and body horror.
Ediction 是为 Love + Attraction Jam 2025 制作的视觉小说。这是一个完整的故事，大约有 23k 字。请注意，它包含并讨论了沉重的主题，尤其是自杀，但也包括死亡、虐待和身体恐怖。
